Research

Research themes

The UHR Lab investigates how urban hydrosystems respond to climate, infrastructure, environmental, governance, and social change.

Urban Flooding, Climate Risk & Infrastructure Performance

Flood exposure, compound hazards, stormwater systems, infrastructure vulnerability, and climate risk assessment.

Urban Water, Environmental Systems & Sustainability

Water affordability, conservation, environmental systems, human-environment interactions, and sustainable planning.

Green Stormwater Infrastructure, Behavior & Adoption

Household GSI adoption, social influence, affordability, latent behavioral factors, and spatial flood exposure.

Governance, Community Knowledge & Decision Support

Citizen science, stakeholder perceptions, governance networks, cross-sector coordination, and adaptation decisions.

Socio-Hydrology & Coupled Human-Water Systems

Feedbacks among people, institutions, infrastructure, water systems, and environmental change.

Geospatial Modeling, Data Science & Risk Analytics

Spatial analysis, remote sensing, network analysis, agent-based modeling, and decision-support workflows.
