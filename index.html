What we do

Research for resilient and sustainable urban water futures.

Our work combines hydrologic modeling, geospatial analysis, agent-based modeling, network analysis, and community-engaged research to understand how cities can better prepare for flooding, infrastructure stress, environmental change, and uneven climate impacts.
