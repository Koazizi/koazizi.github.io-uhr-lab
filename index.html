Azizi Urban Hydrosystems & Resilience Lab

Understanding urban water, environmental, infrastructure, and community systems for climate resilience.

The UHR Lab studies how urban hydrosystems interact with climate risk, environmental change, infrastructure systems, and community decision-making to support resilient and sustainable cities.
